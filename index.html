How To Use
💙 You don't need to do this perfectly. Any time spent doing this together is time well spent.
Before You Start
1
Find a calm, quiet moment.Turn off the TV. Sit close so they can see your face.
2
Make sure the phone isn't on silent.iPhone: no orange stripe on side switch. Android: volume up.
3
Tap any button to wake up the sound.One tap activates audio on all phones before it plays.
Quick Start vs Daily Session
⚡
Quick Start — one tap, goes straight into Beat Reps.Best for days when you just want to go without setup.
🗓
Daily Session — guided Check-In then Beat Reps.Good when you want to log energy and pain levels first.
Beat Reps
1
Choose who's tapping before pressing Play."Caregiver" = you tap. "They tap" = hand them the phone.
2
Tap the pattern card on the beat — the whole card lights up.🟢 On the beat · 🟡 Close · 🔴 Off beat. All attempts count.
3
Star ⭐ patterns worth coming back to.Tap the ☆ in the top right corner to save a favorite.
Groove Mode 🎮
🎮
Notes fall down the screen — tap when they reach the ring.No fail state. No wrong answer. Just groove. Great for visual engagement.
Voice Reps 🎤
🎤
Say each word or number out loud on the beat.Mouthing the words silently still counts. Stop anytime.
Tips
✅ Short sessions 3× a week beat long sessions once a week.
✅ Your voice matters as much as the app. Talk them through each exercise.
✅ It's okay if they don't respond. Being present in the rhythm is still working.
⚠️ Always follow guidance from their medical team. Stop if anything seems wrong.

The pattern card is the tap target
When a pattern plays, tap the big card on the beat. The whole card lights up to show how close the timing was.

🟢 Green = on the beat
🟡 Yellow = close
🔴 Red = off beat
All attempts count. There are no wrong answers here.
